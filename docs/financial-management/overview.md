Successpro's Financial Management module connects contracts, projects, time, expenses, and revenue in one continuous flow. Billing rules are defined upfront and executed automatically, eliminating manual stitching and last-minute reconciliation.

## How billing works

When you create a project in Successpro, you link it to a contract that defines the billing model, rates, and terms. As your team logs time and expenses, those entries flow into the project. Once approved, they're ready for invoicing. Successpro generates invoices directly from approved hours, milestones, usage, and recurring fees, applying all contract terms automatically.

This connected approach means invoices are generated in minutes instead of weeks, and your billing cycle shortens from weeks to days.

## Supported billing models

Successpro supports all major billing structures in a single system:

| Billing Model | Description | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Time & Materials (T&M) | Bill based on actual hours worked at defined rates | Projects with variable scope |
| Fixed Fee | Bill a flat amount for defined deliverables | Well-scoped projects with clear milestones |
| Managed Service | Bill recurring fees for ongoing support or retainers | Long-term client relationships |
| Subscription | Bill recurring fees on a schedule | SaaS-like service offerings |
| Usage-Based | Bill based on consumption metrics (API calls, storage, etc.) | Consumption-driven services |
| Hybrid | Combine multiple models in one contract | Complex engagements with multiple components |

You can manage diverse client contracts without workarounds or external spreadsheets. Each project defines its own billing rules, so you adapt to your customers instead of forcing them into a single model.

## Rate cards

Rate cards define how you price your services. They're the foundation of accurate billing and margin protection.

Create dynamic rate cards aligned to customer contracts or company-wide standards. Price consistently by role, skill, location, level, experience, and currency. Once you set up a rate card, it applies everywhere in the system. Update it once and the change reflects across all projects using that card.

This centralized approach prevents margin leaks from inconsistent pricing and gives you stronger control over your pricing strategy.

:::warning
If you change a rate card mid-project, the change applies to future billing periods only. Historical entries use the rate that was active when they were recorded.
:::

## Contract management

Every project is tied to a contract that governs billing. Successpro stores MSAs, SOWs, POs, and invoices in a structured repository. You can extract key terms like billing model, rates, payment schedule, and milestones directly from contract documents using AI-assisted extraction.

This creates a single source of truth for each engagement. Less reconciliation. Stronger governance.

## Invoicing

Invoices are generated directly from approved time entries, expenses, and project milestones. Discounts, schedules, and adjustments apply automatically based on contract terms.

When you generate an invoice, Successpro pulls all billable work from the project, applies the correct rates, and formats everything according to your contract. You review, add any final adjustments, and send. No manual data entry. No missing line items.

:::tip
Generate invoices as soon as work is approved. The sooner you invoice, the sooner you get paid. Successpro makes this fast enough to do weekly if you want.
:::

## Revenue recognition

Successpro automates revenue recognition using effort-based and milestone percent completion methods. Built-in accrual and deferral reports automatically generate journal entries, keeping you compliant with ASC 606 and IFRS 15.

You don't have to choose between accuracy and compliance. Both happen automatically.
