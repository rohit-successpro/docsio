AI-Powered Insights in Successpro lets you interact with your data conversationally. Instead of building reports or writing queries, you ask questions in plain English and get instant answers.

## Ask a question

1. Go to **Services Analytics** and click **Ask a Question**.
2. Type your question in plain English. Examples:
   - "What's our total revenue this month?"
   - "Which projects are losing money?"
   - "What's our utilization by team?"
   - "Show me revenue at risk."
   - "Which customers are most profitable?"

3. Press Enter or click **Ask**.

Successpro analyzes your data and returns an answer with supporting charts or tables.

## How it works

The AI understands your business structure, roles, permissions, and data relationships. It knows:

- Your projects, customers, and contracts
- Your team members, skills, and costs
- Your revenue, expenses, and margins
- Your billing models and rate cards
- Your allocations and utilization

When you ask a question, the AI translates it into a query against your actual data and returns results in seconds.

:::note
The AI only shows you data you have permission to see. Finance teams see financial metrics. Delivery teams see delivery metrics.
:::

## Generate reports

You can ask the AI to generate reports on demand:

- "Generate a revenue report for Q4"
- "Create a project profitability report"
- "Show me a utilization report by team"
- "Generate a margin analysis by customer"

The AI generates a formatted report with charts, tables, and summary insights. You can download it as PDF or Excel.

## Spot risks early

The AI can flag risks and anomalies in your data:

- **Revenue at risk**: Unconfirmed hours, pending milestones, missing POs
- **Margin drift**: Projects trending toward negative margin
- **Over-allocation**: Team members assigned to more hours than available
- **Bench aging**: Team members on the bench for too long
- **Cost overruns**: Projects exceeding planned costs

Ask the AI to "Show me revenue at risk" or "Flag margin issues" and it will surface problems before they impact your bottom line.

## Model scenarios

You can ask the AI to model the impact of decisions:

- "If we allocate another developer to Project X, what's the margin impact?"
- "What if we increase rates by 10%?"
- "How much would utilization improve if we hired 3 more people?"

The AI models the scenario and shows you the financial impact before you commit.

:::tip
Use scenario modeling to evaluate staffing decisions, pricing changes, and resource allocation before you execute.
:::

## Save insights

When you find an insight you want to keep, save it:

1. Click **Save** on the insight.
2. Enter a **name** (e.g., "Q4 Revenue Forecast").
3. Choose who can **view** it (just you, your team, or everyone).
4. Click **Save**.

Saved insights appear in your **Insights Library** and can be shared with others.

## Conversational follow-ups

You can ask follow-up questions to drill deeper:

1. Ask: "What's our total revenue this month?"
2. AI returns: Total revenue = $500k
3. Follow-up: "Which projects contributed the most?"
4. AI returns: Project breakdown
5. Follow-up: "Show me the details for Project X"
6. AI returns: Project X details

The AI maintains context across follow-ups, so you can have a natural conversation about your data.

## Limitations

The AI can answer questions about:
- Revenue, expenses, and margins
- Projects, customers, and contracts
- Team members, allocations, and utilization
- Billing, invoicing, and payment status
- Delivery status and project health

The AI cannot:
- Make decisions for you (it provides information, not recommendations)
- Access data outside Successpro
- Modify data (it's read-only)
- Predict the future with certainty (it can model scenarios, but not predict)
