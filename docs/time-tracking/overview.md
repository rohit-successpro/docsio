Time tracking in Successpro is designed to eliminate busywork. Instead of building timesheets from scratch, your team reviews and submits pre-filled timesheets. AI extracts the effort from project allocations, task assignments, and work schedules, so data entry is minimal.

## How timesheets work

Timesheets are auto-generated from:
- **Project allocations**: If you're allocated 40 hours/week to a project, the timesheet shows 40 hours.
- **Task assignments**: If you're assigned to specific tasks, the timesheet shows those tasks.
- **Work schedule**: Your regional work schedule (e.g., 40 hours/week, Monday-Friday) determines how many hours appear each week.
- **Time off**: Approved vacation, sick leave, and holidays are excluded from the timesheet.

Your team reviews the pre-filled timesheet, adjusts hours if needed, and submits. No manual entry. No building from scratch.

## Submit a timesheet

1. Go to **Time Tracking** and click **My Timesheet**.
2. Select the **week** or **period** you want to submit.
3. Review the pre-filled entries. Each row shows:
   - **Project** name
   - **Task** (if applicable)
   - **Hours** for each day
   - **Total** hours for the week

4. Adjust hours if needed by clicking on a cell and entering the correct amount.
5. Add additional entries by clicking **Add Entry** and selecting a project and task.
6. Click **Submit for Approval**.

Your manager will review and approve the timesheet. Once approved, the hours flow automatically into project financials and billing.

:::tip
Submit timesheets as soon as the week ends. The sooner you submit, the sooner your manager can approve, and the sooner the hours are available for invoicing.
:::

## Approval workflows

Timesheets flow through multi-level approval workflows. You can configure who approves timesheets and in what order.

### Set up approval workflows

1. Go to **Settings** and click **Approval Workflows**.
2. Click **New Workflow** and select **Timesheet**.
3. Define the **approval levels**:
   - Level 1: Direct manager
   - Level 2: Project manager
   - Level 3: Finance manager

4. Choose **sequential** (each level approves in order) or **parallel** (all levels approve simultaneously).
5. Set **approval deadlines** (e.g., manager must approve within 2 days).
6. Click **Save**.

Now all timesheets follow this workflow. Approvers get notifications and can approve or reject entries.

### Approve a timesheet

1. Go to **Time Tracking** and click **Approvals**.
2. View all timesheets pending your approval.
3. Click a timesheet to review it.
4. Check that hours are accurate and match the work done.
5. Click **Approve** or **Reject**.

If you reject, the submitter gets feedback and can resubmit with corrections.

:::note
Rejected timesheets don't flow into billing. The submitter must correct and resubmit before the hours can be invoiced.
:::

## Compliance and audit trails

Every timesheet action is logged: submission, approval, rejection, and any edits. This creates a complete audit trail for compliance and dispute resolution.

You can view the audit trail by clicking **History** on any timesheet.
