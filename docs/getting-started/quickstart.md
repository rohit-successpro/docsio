This guide walks you through the essential first steps to start using Successpro: creating a project, adding a team member, and capturing time.

## Before you start

You'll need a Successpro account. If you don't have one yet, sign up at [successpro.ai](https://successpro.ai/).

## Create your first project

1. Log in to Successpro and navigate to the **Projects** section.
2. Click **New Project** and enter a project name (e.g., "Client Website Redesign").
3. Select a **billing model** from the dropdown: Time & Materials, Fixed Fee, Managed Service, Subscription, or Usage-Based.
4. Link a **contract** if you have one already set up, or create a simple one by entering the contract amount and duration.
5. Click **Create Project**.

Your project is now live and ready for resource allocation and time tracking.

:::note
You can update billing rules and contract terms anytime. Changes apply to future invoices automatically.
:::

## Add a team member

1. Go to **Resources** and click **Add Team Member**.
2. Enter their name, email, and role (e.g., Developer, Project Manager, Designer).
3. Set their **cost** (hourly rate or annual salary) and **currency**.
4. Assign them to your project by selecting it from the list.
5. Click **Save**.

The team member will receive an invitation email and can start logging time once they accept.

## Capture your first timesheet

1. Navigate to **Time Tracking** and click **My Timesheet**.
2. You'll see a pre-filled timesheet based on your project allocations and work schedule.
3. Review the auto-generated entries and adjust hours if needed.
4. Add any additional hours manually by clicking **Add Entry** and selecting the project and task.
5. Click **Submit for Approval**.

Your manager will review and approve the timesheet. Once approved, the hours flow automatically into project financials and billing.

:::tip
Timesheets are pre-filled from your project allocations and local holidays, so you spend less time on data entry and more time on actual work.
:::

## Generate your first invoice

1. Go to **Financial Management** and click **Invoices**.
2. Select your project and click **Generate Invoice**.
3. Successpro will pull all approved hours, expenses, and milestones from the project.
4. Review the invoice details, apply any discounts or adjustments if needed, and click **Send**.

The invoice is generated in minutes, not weeks. All billing rules from your contract apply automatically.
